This number is used for personal and private messaging only. There is no public opt-in form or marketing campaign.

* The owner initiates communication by forwarding an email to this number or sending a message directly.
* Any individuals who reply to messages do so knowingly, having received a personal message from the number owner.
* In some cases, the number owner initiates a conversation with known contacts who respond affirmatively by text.

**2. Consent and Disclosures**

* Contacts are informed verbally or by message that they may receive alerts from this number.
* They are told they may reply directly or request that messages stop at any time.
* A formal Terms of Service or Privacy Policy is not published due to the non-commercial, personal nature of use.

* Recipients can opt out at any time by texting “STOP” or similar.
* The number owner manually stops sending messages and honors all opt-out requests immediately.
* No further messages are sent to opted-out contacts unless they reinitiate contact or explicitly opt back in.

**4. Record-Keeping**

* Message history is retained in Twilio logs and personal device SMS threads.
* These records serve as documentation of opt-in and opt-out activity if required.

* Messages typically contain personal alerts or email notifications.
* Message frequency is low (generally under 10 messages per day).
* No marketing, mass messaging, or unsolicited communication is performed using this number.
